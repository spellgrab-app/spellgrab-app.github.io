Process

How it works?

Onboarding

We take the video from you along the the languages the video needs to be translated to

Analyzing video

We process the video from the customer and extract lip movements of the actor from the video

Voice Clone

We convert the input audio and clone it to target language, you wont find a difference in orginal and AI generated

Publish

We lip-sync the actor lips according to the AI generated voice and Voila!!

Features

Lip sync

Seamless translation of the audio such that the end product depicts the actors lip syncing the target language

Voice Clone

Translation of videos with the voice cloned into another language, providing the actor’s voice consistently in all the languages

Face-Swap

When the celebrity is unavailable for a shoot, using our technology you can use a dummy actor and then face-swap them for the celebrity’s face. This way, you save your money and celebrity’s time
